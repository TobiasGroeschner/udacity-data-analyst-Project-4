It seems like Retweets and Favorites are highly correlated (red color is a positive correlation). This shows that people who have retweeted have also put a favorites tag. Followers, rating_numerator & rating_denominator do not indicate a significant correlation anywhere.

To confirm this correlation, a scatter plot between Retweets and Favorites was done. This clearly shows a correlation.

One could also do a more sophesticated analysis, e.g. a proper linear regression. But I think visually, the point already becomes clear.

Dog names:

The four favourite dog names are:

  1. Lucy
  2. Charlie
  3. Oliver
  4. Cooper

The None values were exluded.

Dog breed:

The most popular dog breed is golden retriever, with a labrador retriever being the second most popular breed. Chihuahua is not far behind. The page owner could use this data to create target advertis for e.g. food that is suitable for bigger dogs. Or to simply put more content of dogs which seem to be particularly popular to create more traffic on the site.

Favorite vs Retweet counts:

There is a positive correlation between "like" counts and how much a post was retweeted. Interactions on the site are driven with retweets so it of interest of Twitter to make tweets that create interaction more visible.
